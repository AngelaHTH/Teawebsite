Refund Quaries

What is your refund policy?

    Our current refund policy is the item must be in good condition and not open. Our refund period is within 14 days after purchase.

How do i request for refund?

    Customer can either fill up the feedback form or contact us for any enquires.via our contact us page.

How long does the refund process takes?

    Normally, our refund process would taking 3-7 working days. If you did not received your refund after 14 working day feel free to contact us.

Are there any item that are not refundable?

    Nope! All of out item is refundable.

Do i need to state the reason for refunding an item?

    Yes! After contacting you for your refund, we would require you to state the reason for your refund. Doing so would assist in understanding how can we improve our product

What is your exhange policy?

    We would require customer to contact us via our contact us page. Customer are also to ensure that the item is in good condition and the item is not open.

Do i need to return the item to get an refund?

    You do not have to return the item that you have refunded to us.
