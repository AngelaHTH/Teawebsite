Tea workshop

Beyond tea sampling lies a profound history and philosophy of the ancient art of tea appreciation, which we believe is best uncovered through learning. Whether you are a tea sommelier or simply curious about tea, Tea Garden believes that anyone can learn and appreciate its fascinating culture.

These private sessions are specially designed to engage our customer through a personalised and tailored delivery of instruction. Our tea master will guide you through the proper water temperature, steeping times, and brewing techniques for a perfect cup. Discover how these elements influence the final flavor and aroma of your tea.

After mastering the art of brewing, gather around and savor a variety of teas. Learn how to identify subtle flavor profiles and appreciate the unique characteristics of each type. Enjoy these delightful beverages with delicious accompaniments, creating a truly memorable tea experience.
